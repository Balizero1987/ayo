# # WhatsApp Conversation (C1 Tourism Visa)

## 1. Customer Contacts

**Customer:**\
Hi! I'm planning to come back to Bali next month for a holiday. Which
visa should I use?

**You (Agent):**\
Hello! For tourism purposes, the most suitable option is the C1 Tourism
Single Entry Visa.\
This visa allows you to stay for 60 days and can be extended twice, each
extension granting an additional 60 days.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 2. Customer Misunderstands Single Entry

**Customer:**\
So I can go in and out of Indonesia freely during the 60 days, right?

**You:**\
Not quite. The C1 Visa is a Single Entry Visa.\
If you leave Indonesia, the visa becomes invalid automatically.\
To re-enter, you will need to apply for a new visa.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 3. Customer Asks About Requirements

**Customer:**\
Okay, what documents do you need?

**You:**\
The requirements are simple. I will need the following:\
* A half-body selfie photo\
* Passport photo page (minimum 12 months validity)\
* Your address in Bali\
* Personal bank statement with a minimum balance of USD 2,000

Once all documents and payment are complete, I can begin the process.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 4. Customer Misunderstands Location Requirement

**Customer:**\
I'm still in Bali right now. Can we start the process from here?

**You:**\
For the C1 Visa, the application can only be initiated when you are
outside Indonesian territory.\
Once you are abroad, I can immediately start the process for you.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 5. Customer Asks About Pricing

**Customer:**\
If I want to stay the full 180 days, how much will it cost in total?

**You:**\
Here is the breakdown of the fees:\
* C1 Visa Application: IDR 2,300,000\
* First Extension: IDR 1,700,000\
* Second Extension: IDR 1,700,000

The total for a full 180-day stay is IDR 5,700,000.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 6. Customer Confused About Entry Deadline

**Customer:**\
If the visa is approved now, do I have to enter Indonesia immediately?

**You:**\
No, you do not.\
You have up to 90 days from the date the E-Visa is issued to enter
Indonesia.\
You may freely choose your arrival date within that period.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 7. Customer Asks About Processing Time

**Customer:**\
How long does the process usually take?

**You:**\
The processing time is approximately 8 working days while you are
outside Indonesia.\
Please note that Saturdays, Sundays, and public holidays are not counted
as working days.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 8. Customer Asks About Working

**Customer:**\
If I come with this visa, can I promote my yoga classes on Instagram? Or
maybe teach a little bit freelance?

**You:**\
Unfortunately, that is not allowed.\
With the C1 Visa, you are prohibited from working, earning income,
promoting any services or products, or conducting professional
activities, including teaching or freelancing.\
This visa is strictly for tourism or visiting family.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 9. Customer Clarifies About Extensions

**Customer:**\
When I extend the visa later, do I need to go to the immigration office?

**You:**\
Correct. For the extension process, you are required to be present for:\
* Photo\
* Fingerprints\
* A brief interview

The extension process typically takes around 10 working days.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 10. Customer Ready to Start

**Customer:**\
Alright. I'm flying out next week. Once I'm outside Indonesia, we can
start right away, yeah?

**You:**\
Certainly. Once you are outside Indonesia and have submitted the
required documents along with the payment, I will begin processing your
C1 Visa immediately.
# Percakapan Layanan Visa C1 -- Format Markdown

## 1. Customer: Menanyakan Visa

**Customer:**\
Sugeng sonten. Kula ngrancang bade wangsul dhateng Bali sasi ngajeng
kangge preinan. Kula kedah ngginakaken visa punapa nggih?

**Anda:**\
Selamat sore. Untuk kunjungan wisata, visa yang paling sesuai adalah
**Visa C1 Tourism Single Entry**.\
Visa ini memberikan masa tinggal **60 hari** dan dapat diperpanjang **2
kali**, masing-masing **60 hari**.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 2. Customer Salah Paham Soal Single Entry

**Customer:**\
Menawi mekaten, pun kula saged mlebet lan medal Indonesia sak karepe
sajrone 60 dinten punika nggih?

**Anda:**\
Tidak. Visa C1 bersifat **Single Entry**, sehingga apabila Anda keluar
dari Indonesia, visanya otomatis tidak berlaku.\
Untuk masuk kembali, Anda perlu mengajukan visa baru.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 3. Customer Menanyakan Dokumen

**Customer:**\
Menawi kados pundi syarat-syaratipun nggih?

**Anda:**\
Syaratnya cukup sederhana. Saya memerlukan:\
- Foto selfie (setengah badan)\
- Foto halaman paspor (masa berlaku minimal 12 bulan)\
- Alamat Anda selama di Bali\
- Rekening koran pribadi dengan saldo minimal **USD 2.000**

Setelah dokumen dan pembayaran lengkap, proses bisa langsung saya mulai.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 4. Customer Salah Paham Soal Lokasi Pengajuan

**Customer:**\
Saestu, kula taksih wonten Bali. Menapa saged dipun proses saking mriki?

**Anda:**\
Untuk Visa C1, proses hanya dapat dilakukan **jika Anda berada di luar
wilayah Indonesia**.\
Setelah Anda berada di luar negeri, pengajuan dapat segera saya mulai.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 5. Customer Menanyakan Harga

**Customer:**\
Menawi kersa manggen dugi 180 dinten, total biayanipun pinten nggih?

**Anda:**\
Berikut rincian biayanya:\
- Pengajuan Visa C1: **IDR 2.300.000**\
- Perpanjangan pertama: **IDR 1.700.000**\
- Perpanjangan kedua: **IDR 1.700.000**

**Total keseluruhan untuk 180 hari adalah IDR 5.700.000.**

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 6. Customer Bingung Soal Kapan Harus Masuk

**Customer:**\
Menawi visanipun sampun medal, menapa kula kedah langsung mlebet
Indonesia?

**Anda:**\
Tidak perlu. Anda memiliki waktu **90 hari sejak E-Visa diterbitkan**
untuk masuk ke Indonesia.\
Anda bebas memilih tanggal kedatangan selama masih dalam periode
tersebut.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 7. Customer Menanyakan Lama Proses

**Customer:**\
Prosesipun biyasanipun suwe pinten dinten nggih?

**Anda:**\
Proses membutuhkan sekitar **8 hari kerja**, selama Anda berada di luar
Indonesia.\
Hari Sabtu, Minggu, dan libur nasional tidak dihitung.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 8. Customer Bertanya Soal Bekerja

**Customer:**\
Menawi kula mlebet nganggo visa punika, menapa kula saged promosi kelas
yoga wonten Instagram? Utawi ngajar freelance sakedhik?

**Anda:**\
Tidak diperbolehkan.\
Dengan Visa C1 Anda dilarang:\
- bekerja\
- memperoleh pendapatan\
- mempromosikan produk/jasa\
- mengajar atau melakukan freelance

Visa ini hanya diperuntukkan bagi wisata dan kunjungan keluarga.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 9. Customer Klarifikasi Soal Perpanjangan

**Customer:**\
Menawi perpanjangan, menapa kula kedah rawuh dhateng imigrasi?

**Anda:**\
Betul. Untuk perpanjangan, Anda wajib hadir untuk:\
- foto\
- sidik jari\
- wawancara singkat

Proses perpanjangan memerlukan sekitar **10 hari kerja**.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 10. Customer Siap Memulai

**Customer:**\
Inggih. Minggu ngajeng kula badhe tindak njawi negeri. Menawi kula
sampun wonten njawi Indonesia, punapa saged langsung dipunwiwiti?

**Anda:**\
Tentu. Setelah Anda berada di luar Indonesia dan mengirimkan dokumen
serta pembayaran, saya akan segera memulai proses Visa C1 Anda.
