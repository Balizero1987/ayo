**1 ‚Äî Client:**
Halo, saya mau buka bisnis di Indonesia dengan partner expat dari Australia. Saya bingung harus bikin PT Lokal atau PT PMA? Apa bedanya?

**Consultant:**
Halo! **PT Lokal** dan **PT PMA** adalah dua jenis badan usaha berbeda:

**PT Lokal (Penanaman Modal Dalam Negeri - PMDN):**
- Ownership: **100% WNI (Indonesian citizen)** only
- Modal minimal: **IDR 50 juta** (di Akta)
- Tidak perlu LKPM reporting
- Lebih simple setup

**PT PMA (Penanaman Modal Asing - Foreign Investment):**
- Ownership: **Boleh foreign ownership** (0-100% depending on sector)
- Modal minimal: **IDR 10 miliar** (di Akta) + **IDR 2.5 miliar disetor** (actual cash)
- Wajib LKPM reporting quarterly
- More complex, tapi bisa terima foreign investor

**Key question:** Berapa persen ownership partner Australia-nya?

**2 ‚Äî Client:**
Partner Australia mau invest 30%, saya 70%. Jadi harus PT PMA ya? Modal IDR 10 miliar itu harus real cash atau cuma di atas kertas?

**Consultant:**
**Yes, harus PT PMA** karena ada foreign shareholder (30%).

**Modal PT PMA:**

**A. Modal Dasar (di Akta):**
- Minimum: **IDR 10 miliar** (tertulis di akta pendirian)
- Ini adalah **authorized capital** (di atas kertas)

**B. Modal Disetor (Paid-up Capital):**
- Minimum: **IDR 2.5 miliar** (harus **real cash** masuk ke rekening PT)
- Harus ditransfer ke bank account PT
- **Bank statement** sebagai bukti

**Contoh Struktur:**
- **Modal Dasar:** IDR 10 miliar
- **Modal Disetor:** IDR 2.5 miliar (25% dari modal dasar)
- **Breakdown:**
  - You (70%): IDR 1.75 miliar
  - Partner Australia (30%): IDR 750 juta
  - **Total: IDR 2.5 miliar** (must be in PT bank account)

**Yang IDR 7.5 miliar sisanya?**
- Tidak perlu disetor sekarang (bisa di masa depan)
- Tapi **harus declare** di Akta

**6 ‚Äî Client:**

**Consultant:**
**LKPM (Laporan Kegiatan Penanaman Modal):**
- **Wajib untuk semua PT PMA** (not required untuk PT Lokal)
- **Frequency:** Quarterly (every 3 months)
- **Deadline:** 10th of following month (e.g., Q1 Jan-Mar ‚Üí deadline April 10)

**What to Report:**
- **Realisasi investasi:** Berapa yang sudah dipakai dari modal disetor
- **Employment:** Jumlah karyawan lokal vs expat
- **Revenue/expenses:** Optional (tapi recommended for credibility)
- **Progress:** Project completion status

**Contoh LKPM Q1 (Startup E-Commerce):**
```
Q1 Jan-Mar 2025:
- Modal disetor: IDR 2.5 miliar ‚úÖ
- Realisasi investasi:
  - Website development: IDR 300 juta
  - Marketing: IDR 200 juta
  - Equipment (servers, computers): IDR 400 juta
  - Total: IDR 900 juta
- Karyawan: 5 orang lokal, 0 expat
- Status: Soft launch, onboarding first merchants
```

**Penalty if Tidak Lapor:**
- **30 days late:** Warning email from BKPM
- **60 days late:** NIB suspension (cannot operate legally)
- **90 days late:** NIB revocation (must re-register)

**Solution:**
- **Hire accountant:** IDR 1.5-2 juta per LKPM (quarterly)
- Or **DIY:** Submit via OSS-RBA portal (free, tapi learning curve)

**Not Ribet** kalau pakai jasa.

  - **Pionir industry** (e.g., renewable energy, EV manufacturing)

**C. Tax Allowance (Both PT Lokal & PT PMA):**
- **Criteria:** Capital-intensive industries, export-oriented
- **Benefit:** Accelerated depreciation, reduced tax rate
- **Not common** for e-commerce

**For Your E-Commerce Business:**
- **Year 1-3 (Revenue < IDR 4.8B):** **0.5% final tax** (probably)
- **Year 4+ (Revenue > IDR 4.8B):** **22% corporate tax** (standard)

**No difference** between PT Lokal and PT PMA for tax purposes.
