WHAT HAPPENED?

Badung Regency launched a 1-month traffic experiment in Kerobokan, starting December 14, 2025.

The goal? Reduce congestion before 2.8 million tourists hit the island for Christmas and New Year.

The timing? Legendary.

Same weekend: Cyclone 93S.
Floods in Seminyak, Kerobokan, Denpasar.
Viral gridlock videos everywhere.

You have to admire the confidence.
